About the Tournament

The Nathan Thomas 6v6 Fall Tournament, started by Laurelhurst resident Sue Ofstad the summer after Nathan died, gave young recreational teams in the Portland area an opportunity to compete in a competitive but friendly tournament. Over the years hundreds of children and adults participated in the tournament as players and volunteers. It was more than a soccer tournament, it was a celebration of children, families and neighbors working and playing together with a shared community heart.

History

Spirit of Portland Award

In 2001 Sue Ofstad, tournament founder and director since 1992, was presented with a Spirit of Portland Individual Award for her contributions to the community through the tournament.
